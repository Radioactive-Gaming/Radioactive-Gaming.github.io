The RADZ Community Staff

Organization Chart

Senior Staff

Captain Undies

Radioactive Gaming Clan Founder and Leader

Since its inception in 1996, Captain Undies has led the Radioactive Gaming clan through thick and thin. She is a firm believer in community and leadership, which are two values that the RADZ crew take seriously. While she no longer runs operations directly, she has a firm policy that her door is always open to those who need an ear. Outside of the RADZ community, Cap spends most of her time exploring the great outdoors, experiencing different cultures or working on one of her two Jeeps.

Sheepy049

Captain of RADZ Community, Director of Operations

Sheepy joined us during the 2014 RADZ revival and quickly became a community favorite with his fun, but fair, attitude towards people. Over the last few years, he has worked his way up the ranks to become the first clan member to be promoted to captain since 2003. Sheepy’s primary focus is overall operations and improvements of the RADZ community, as well as recruiting for the Radioactive Gaming clan.

Doctor Moose

Director of Communications

Moose joined us in early 2016 as one of our Team Fortress 2 community regulars and his amiable demeanor quickly lead to his membership in the Radioactive Gaming clan. During the 2016 reorganization effort, Moose stepped up to help run the ship and now leads the efforts in community collaboration, including our VoIP chat technologies and various web sites.

Server Team

AstroC

Plug.DJ Administrator

Astro stumbled upon our community when Captain Undies and Tzameti invaded the HORAM Plug room. Ever since then, Astro has been an active and dedicated member of the Radioactive Gaming clan. Known for his passion and knowledge of music, he always brings new topics of discussion into the fold. Astro’s focus is on our Plug.DJ room’s programming, staff and recruitment.

IllusiveConman

Killing Floor Administrator

Conman joined us in early 2015 after a night of mayhem in GTA V. Reigning from the other side of the pond, his previous experience as a Gary’s Mod administrator and Discord moderator made him a perfect fit for the RADZ staff.

REDCHICKEN

Counter-Strike Administrator

Chicken joined us in 2015 during a night of Counter-Strike and Team Fortress shenanigans. Over time, he has proven himself to be a loyal and committed member of the Radioactive Gaming clan. With his leadership skills and his knowledge in game play, Chicken is responsible for server management and recruiting in the Counter-Strike community.

Communications Team

SectorJay

Forum Developer and Administrator

In early 2014, Jay made his presence known during a Cactus Canyon brawl and was swiftly recruited into the clan. His motivation, dedication, honesty and spontaneous personality earned him a spot in the foundation of the community. Jay’s primary focus is the development and administration of the communities various forums.
